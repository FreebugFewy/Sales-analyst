# Sales Enablement AI Solutions Analyst Dashboard — Design Spec

**Date:** 2026-04-20
**Role:** Sales Enablement AI Solutions Analyst — Visa NA VAS (North America Visa Acceptance Solutions)
**Project type:** Cloned repo, Streamlit multipage app (4 pages)

---

## Overview

A 4-page Streamlit dashboard that models the analytical and AI automation work performed by a Sales Enablement AI Solutions Analyst embedded in Visa's NA VAS team. The portfolio covers Visa's real Acceptance Solutions products: Tap-to-Phone, Tokenization Suite, Acceptance API, and Visa Acceptance Console — sold to Tier 1 Banks, Regional Banks, ISOs, and Fintechs.

The four pages map directly to the four pillars in the job description: Analytics & Insights, Pipeline Velocity / GTM Strategy, Post-Sales Time-to-Revenue Acceleration, and AI & Automation. The centrepiece is the AI Insight Engine on Page 4 — a rule-based recommendation system that surfaces next-best-actions for stalled deals, demonstrating the automation capability the JD explicitly prioritises.

## Pure Functions

### `compute_deal_health(win_rate, avg_days_to_close, mom_growth) → (float, str)`

**File:** `streamlit_app/utils/deal_health.py`

Weighted composite score (0–100):
- Win rate: 45%
- MoM pipeline growth: 30%
- Days-to-close efficiency (inverted — lower days = higher score): 25%

Returns a float score and a band: `Strong` (≥70), `Moderate` (40–69), `At Risk` (<40).

No Streamlit dependency. Fully unit-testable.

### `compute_ai_recommendations(deals_df) → list[dict]`

**File:** `streamlit_app/utils/ai_recommendations.py`

Scans the `deals.csv` snapshot and flags deals where:
- Deal stalled in current pre-sale stage > 21 days
- Deal stalled in current post-sale stage > 14 days
- Post-sale deals in Onboarding or Certification with no stage progression for 10+ days

Returns a list of dicts: `{deal_id, product, acquirer_segment, stage, flag_type, severity, next_best_action}`.

Severity levels: `Critical` (post-sale stall >14 days), `Warning` (pre-sale stall >21 days or post-sale stall 10–14 days), `Watch` (approaching thresholds).

The `next_best_action` field is an exec-ready one-line string (e.g. "Tap-to-Phone deal with First National stalled in Certification for 18 days — schedule technical readiness call with acquirer integration team.").

No Streamlit dependency. Fully unit-testable.

## Pages

### Page 1 — Executive Pipeline Summary (`1_pipeline_summary.py`)

**JD Pillar:** Analytics & Insights

**Purpose:** Monday-morning pipeline health view for a sales leader.

- **KPI cards (top row):** Total pipeline value (USD), blended win rate %, avg days to close, MoM pipeline growth (with delta vs prior month)
- **Pipeline trend chart:** Monthly pipeline value by VAS product (Altair line chart, last 12 months)
- **Deal Health Scorecard table:** Each product's latest health score, band (Strong/Moderate/At Risk), win rate, avg deal size, MoM growth — sorted by score descending, colour-coded by band

### Page 2 — Pipeline Velocity Model (`pages/2_pipeline_velocity.py`)

**JD Pillar:** GTM Strategy & Prioritisation

**Purpose:** Surface what drives deal speed and conversion across products and segments.

- **Filters:** Product dropdown + acquirer segment multiselect
- **Win rate over time:** Bar chart by month, coloured by attainment band
- **Days-to-close by segment:** Horizontal bar chart (latest 3-month avg) — shows which acquirer segments close fastest
- **Velocity Score Model:** Altair scatter of avg deal size vs. win rate by segment, with trend line. Key insight: ISOs close faster at lower deal size; Tier 1 Banks carry higher value but longer cycles — informs GTM prioritisation

### Page 3 — Post-Sale Time-to-Revenue (`pages/3_time_to_revenue.py`)

**JD Pillar:** Post-Sales Intake, Handoffs & Time-to-Revenue Acceleration

**Purpose:** Show where post-sale deals stall and quantify the revenue delay cost.

- **Stage funnel:** Horizontal bar showing deal count at each post-sale stage (Intake → Onboarding → Certification → Activation → Live)
- **Avg cycle time per stage:** Bar chart of mean days per stage — bottleneck stages flagged red when avg days exceeds threshold (Certification > 14 days, Onboarding > 10 days)
- **Time-to-revenue by product:** Distribution of days from Closed Won to Live, grouped by VAS product

### Page 4 — AI Insight Engine (`pages/4_ai_insight_engine.py`)

**JD Pillar:** AI, Automation & Workflow Enablement

**Purpose:** Automate the manual work of identifying stalled deals and surfacing next-best-actions — the kind of tool the JD describes as "eliminating manual work and improving efficiency."

- **Severity KPI cards:** Count of Critical / Warning / Watch flags across all deals
- **Recommendations table:** All flags from `compute_ai_recommendations()` with deal ID, product, segment, stage, severity, and next-best-action — expandable rows
- **Strategic Priorities:** Static exec briefing block — 3 prioritised recommendations formatted as a QBR talking point (e.g. Certification bottleneck costing estimated X days of revenue per deal, ISO win rate declining vs. prior quarter, Tap-to-Phone onboarding gap in Regional Bank segment)

## Testing Strategy

Both pure functions are tested in isolation with no Streamlit dependency.

**`test_deal_health.py`** (~6 tests):
- Strong/Moderate/At Risk band boundaries
- Correct weighting (win-rate-dominant vs growth-dominant inputs)
- Edge cases: all zeros, all ones, very high days-to-close

**`test_ai_recommendations.py`** (~6 tests):
- Flags correctly when pre-sale deal stalled > 21 days
- Flags correctly when post-sale deal stalled > 14 days
- Correct severity assignment (Critical vs Warning vs Watch)
- No false positives on healthy, fast-moving deals
- `next_best_action` string is non-empty for every flag
